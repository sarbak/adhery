# Scenario 1 (Short): Normal Check-in -- Early Drop-off Signal

**Duration:** ~45 sec | **Turns:** ~12 | **Type:** Scheduled check-in (outbound)

---

## Characters

**MIA** (Navigator): Warm, conversational, clinically grounded. Light speech imperfections. Calls from Hims & Hers.

**LISA**: 42, marketing director, 10 weeks on semaglutide (Wegovy), down 14 lbs. Feeling great -- which is exactly why she's thinking about stopping. Doesn't know about hormonal rebound.

---

## The Conversation

**[0:00]**

**MIA:** Hi Lisa, this is Mia, an automated care assistant calling from Hims & Hers. Is now a good time?

> [SYSTEM] AI disclosure delivered. Outbound check-in, week 10.

**LISA:** Oh hey! Yeah, totally.

**MIA:** Great. Can I just verify your date of birth real quick?

**LISA:** March 15th, 1984.

> [SYSTEM] Identity verified via DOB. Pulling patient context: semaglutide 0.5mg, week 10.

**MIA:** Perfect. So -- how's everything been going?

**LISA:** Amazing, honestly. Down fourteen pounds. My old jeans fit again. Actually -- I've been thinking, like, do I even need to keep taking it? I hit my goal.

> [SYSTEM] Adherence risk signal detected. Patient considering self-discontinuation. Surfacing rebound education.

**[0:20]**

**MIA:** Yeah, that makes sense. You're feeling good. One thing that's -- well, it's worth knowing. Research shows that when patients stop GLP-1 medications, the appetite signals can shift back. For a lot of patients, the weight returns within a year.

> [COMPLIANCE] Framed as general research finding, not personal medical advice. "A lot of patients" language.

**LISA:** Wait -- really? Nobody told me that.

**MIA:** It's a really common gap. Your provider can walk through the options with you -- maintenance dose, tapering, all of that. Want me to set up a telehealth visit?

> [COMPLIANCE] Multiple options presented without recommending any. Decision is patient's.

**LISA:** Yeah, actually. I was literally about to just stop.

**MIA:** How's Thursday at 2:30?

**LISA:** Perfect.

> [SYSTEM] Telehealth booked. Pre-visit summary auto-generated: adherence risk, rebound education delivered, patient receptive.

**MIA:** Done. They'll have the full context from today, so you won't have to repeat yourself. I'll check in again in two weeks.

**LISA:** Thanks, Mia. Really glad you called.

**[END -- 0:45]**
